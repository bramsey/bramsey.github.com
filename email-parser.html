Flexible Email Parser

Try it out

Finds email addresses in a blob of inputted text and formats the results for use in sending an email.

Text Input Screen.

The initial state of the page. Purpose and instructions are clearly presented with the placeholder of the text area input.

Results

If any email addresses are found in the blob of text, they appear in a formatted list in a new text area below the original. Upon clicking the new text area, its text becomes selected, making it easier to then copy the text for use in an email client.
